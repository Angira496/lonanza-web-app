Customer Demographics & Loan Status Analysis

The loan approval analysis reveals that financial stability and demographic factors significantly influence lending decisions. Graduates, non-self-employed individuals, and those with strong credit histories have higher approval rates, likely due to better earning potential and consistent incomes. Semiurban residents see more approvals, while males apply more, though gender has minimal impact on outcomes. Married applicants and those without dependents are favored, reflecting perceived financial reliability. Most applicants are graduates with good credit and reside in varied property areas. Additionally, the distributions of incomes and loan amounts are positively skewed with outliers, while loan amount term is negatively skewed, also containing outliers.
